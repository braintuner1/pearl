International Partners

Global organizations supporting our mission

Strategic Partner

United Nations

Through UNESCO and UNICEF, the United Nations supports our educational initiatives with technical expertise, funding for school infrastructure, and capacity building programs for teachers and administrators. Their Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Quality Education) aligns perfectly with our mission.

Funding Technical Support Capacity Building
Funding Partner

World Bank Group

The World Bank provides critical funding through their Education for All initiative, supporting our scholarship programs and educational infrastructure development. Their impact assessment frameworks help us measure and improve our program effectiveness.

Scholarships Infrastructure Research

Government & Ministry Partners

National institutions supporting education

Implementation Partner
Ministry of Education & Sports

Our primary government partner providing policy support, curriculum alignment, and access to public school systems. They facilitate our programs' integration with national education standards.

Policy Support Curriculum
Local Government
Kampala Capital City Authority

KCCA supports our urban education initiatives, providing access to city schools and facilities for our programs. They help identify and reach non-teaching staff families within Kampala.

Urban Programs Facilities
UN Agency
Uganda National Commission for UNESCO

Supports our teacher training programs and promotes inclusive education practices. They provide expertise in educational quality assurance and international best practices.

Teacher Training Quality Assurance
