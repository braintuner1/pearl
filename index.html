Our Story

Pearl Edu Fund (PEF), Student-led education initiative

Pearl Edu Fund (PEF) is a student-led, non-profit initiative devoted to promoting equal access to education for children from underprivileged families, particularly those of school support (non-teaching) staff. Founded in 2022 at St. Mary’s College Kisubi, PEF mobilizes students, volunteers and partners to provide scholarships, scholastic materials, tutoring and mentorship so every child can access quality education.

About Volunteering

Join Pearl Edu Fund as a volunteer to support our mission of financing education for children of non-teaching staff. Volunteers assist with tutoring and mentorship, organising school-supply drives, beneficiary selection, fundraising events and basic administration. Commitments are flexible — help for a single event or sign up for an ongoing role.
